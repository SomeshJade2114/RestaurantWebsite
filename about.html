About Food Delight


Food Delight is your go-to spot for discovering delightful meals. We offer a variety of dishes that cater to all tastes! Food Delight is an Indian online food ordering and delivery company. Founded in 2019, Food Delight is headquartered in Bangalore and operates in more than 580 Indian cities, as of July 2024. Besides food delivery, the platform also provides quick commerce services under the name Food Delight Instamart, and same-day package deliveries with Food Delight Genie.
